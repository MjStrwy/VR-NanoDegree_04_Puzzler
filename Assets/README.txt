Project: Puzzler VR

Description: A medieval dungeon where the player interacts with magical orbs to complete a puzzle and escape the dungeon. 

Instructions: Use the orbs by highlighting them with your reticle, and press the Cardboard button to select the orb.

Author: Russell Alleen-Willems

Build Date: 9/?/2017

Platforms: Google Cardboard / Android

Software Versions: Unity 2017.1.0p4, GVR 1.60.0
